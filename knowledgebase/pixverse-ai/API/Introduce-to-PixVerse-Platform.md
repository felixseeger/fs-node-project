# Introduce to PixVerse Platform



### **What is PixVerse?**
:::highlight purple 💡
- PixVerse has taken social media by storm with creative effects like "Venom Transformation", providing millions of users with an unprecedented AI video generation experience.
- Currently offering text-to-video, image-to-video, special effects, and various AI video generation capabilities to global users, it represents the world's leading AI video generation technology.
- Visit PixVerse Platform: [https://platform.pixverse.ai/](https://platform.pixverse.ai?utm_source=apidocs&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=normal)
