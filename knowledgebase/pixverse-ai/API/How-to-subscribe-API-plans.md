## How to Subscribe


### Step 1: Choose a Plan

1. Log in to your PixVerse account at [https://platform.pixverse.ai/](https://platform.pixverse.ai?utm_source=apidocs&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=normal)
2. Navigate to "Billing" in the left sidebar
 [https://platform.pixverse.ai/billing](https://platform.pixverse.ai/billing?utm_source=apidocs&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=normal)
3. Review the available plans and their features
4. Select the plan that best fits your needs

### Step 2: Complete Payment Information
1. Click "Subscribe" on your chosen plan
2. Enter your payment details
3. Confirm your subscription

### Step 3: Manage Your Subscription

After subscribing, you can:
- View your current usage and remaining credits
- Cancel plan
