**Brand Assets Fusion – Creative Brief**

**1. Project Overview**
Craft a unified visual identity piece that seamlessly merges all four brand assets into a single cohesive composition, where each element earns its place and amplifies the others.

Think of the composition as a living ecosystem. The model is the human heartbeat, the icon is the soul, the color palette is the atmosphere, and the typography is the voice. Together, they should feel like one organism, not four parts.
